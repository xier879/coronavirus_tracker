Coronavirus Tracker Project

Presented by Minchan Choi, Roaya Helal, Nathan Mace, Xier Zhao


Our project focus on collecting and visuliazing data related to Coronavirus cases including the death rates on a worldwide scale over periods. Since the first case has been identified in Wuhan, China, Coronavirus has been furiously spreading to the whole world only in a couple of months.

Human beings still have little knowledge about what cause, how treat to the novel coronavirus at this stage.After the first known U.S. coronavarius case on Jan. 21, America cases hit to over 1000 cases. (Updated on March 11th,2020).

We would like to show the public audience that where and how severe has been hit hard by the virus
